Food


Delicious Meals To Try

Paella


Paella is a traditional Spanish rice dish that originated in Valencia, typically made with saffron-flavored rice, vegetables, and ingredients like chicken, rabbit, or seafood. It is one of Spain's most famous and iconic dishes.

Tapas


Tapas are small Spanish dishes or appetizers, traditionally shared and served with drinks, offering a variety of flavors ranging from olives and cheeses to seafood and meats.

Churros


Churros are a popular Spanish fried-dough pastry, usually dusted with sugar and often served with thick hot chocolate for dipping.
